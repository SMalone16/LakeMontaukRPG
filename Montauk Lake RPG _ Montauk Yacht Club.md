**2 Area Overview |**  Montauk Yacht Club

**Description:**  
The club occupies all 35 acres of Star Island, a pine-fringed spit linked to Montauk’s mainland by a low causeway. From the lighthouse balcony you can watch charter boats idle through the harbor at dawn, smell brine and sunscreen drifting from the private beach by noon, and see the sky glaze pink behind hundreds of polished transoms at dusk. Inside, cedar-shingle cottages, white-wash brick, and navy-striped deck chairs keep the visual tone “classic Hamptons” rather than hyper-luxury nightclub. Fun, leisurely activities to take part in throughout the day.
